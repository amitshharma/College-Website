Creative Arts

Creative Arts We boast a proven record for creative and scholarly outputs of international excellence in the field of the arts. Research takes place within the School and in collaboration with UK and global research partners, investigating new directions in both the creation and the analysis of a wide range of cultural forms.

School and department research strengths.

Each of the University’s academic schools has distinctive research strengths. A key characteristic of the research activity across our Loughborough and London campuses is the way in which we apply expertise across disciplines, in partnership with a breadth of external organisations to create far reaching impact and influence.

-ParaSports-

Creating more opportunities in ParaSports.

Our research partnerships have also improved the number of sporting opportunities for people with disabilities worldwide. Working with international partners as well as GB teams, researchers from Oak Park's Peter Harrison Centre for Disability Sport have played a critical role in shaping new events and rules as well as safeguarding the health of Para-athletes.
