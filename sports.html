Sports

We are proud of our hard-earned reputation for sport at Oak Park, but our constant focus is on esuring every student and member of staff has the opportunity to get involved, regardless of ability or interest.

Whether you want to try something new or are dedicated to your chosen sport, there are always lots of opportunities to get involved. From Ultimate Frisbee, Equestrian and Sub Aqua AU Clubs to performance Rugby, Cricket and Football, Hall sports and societies, there’s something for everyone.

It’s your passion and ambition that creates the atmosphere of determination on campus and contributes to our renowned student experience. We hope you enjoy being part of sport at Oak Park.
